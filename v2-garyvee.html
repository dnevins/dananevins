The Story

Twenty-five years ago, I was elbow-deep in a 1970 Bronco restoration, buying and trading parts through what passed for ecommerce in the late '90s. The technology was terrible. So I built something better. Web Shop Manager started as a fix for one friend's online parts store and became the infrastructure behind over a thousand aftermarket businesses. In 2020, I started PartsLogic to push the boundaries further — smarter search, better fitment, modern developer tools. Every day, I'm still solving the same fundamental problem: how do you make it easy to sell complex parts online?
