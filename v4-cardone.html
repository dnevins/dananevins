THE MAN BEHIND THE MISSION

In the late 1990s, Dana Nevins was restoring a 1970 Ford Bronco when he stumbled onto something bigger than a hobby — a massive gap in how the automotive aftermarket sold parts online. The technology was primitive. The user experience was painful. The opportunity was enormous.

In 2000, he founded Web Shop Manager and spent the next two decades building it into the aftermarket’s most trusted ecommerce platform. In 2020, he launched PartsLogic to push the industry even further — smarter search, better fitment, modern developer tools.

Today, Dana’s technology powers over a thousand businesses and has processed more than a billion dollars in transactions across auto, truck, powersports, marine, and firearms.
