The Backstory

It started with a 1970 Bronco, a driveway, and a hunch. Trading parts with Jeff's Bronco Graveyard in the late '90s, I saw firsthand how badly the automotive aftermarket needed better ecommerce tools. In 2000, I built Web Shop Manager to fill that gap. Twenty-five years later, it powers over a thousand stores and has processed more than a billion dollars in transactions. In 2020, I launched PartsLogic to give developers and agencies modern, API-first tools for the next generation of automotive ecommerce.
