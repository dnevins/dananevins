The Backstory

It all started with a rusted-out 1970 Ford Bronco and a driveway full of ambition. Trading parts online with Jeff's Bronco Graveyard in the late '90s, I discovered something the automotive aftermarket desperately lacked — real ecommerce technology built for the way this industry actually works. In 2000, I founded Web Shop Manager to fix that. Twenty-five years and over a billion dollars in processed transactions later, we've helped more than a thousand businesses transform complex, high-volume product catalogs into their greatest competitive weapon.

What I'm Building

Web Shop Manager
